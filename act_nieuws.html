DIR 2012

The primary aim of DIR 2012 is to provide an international meeting place where researchers from the domain of information retrieval and related disciplines can exchange information and present innovative research developments. DIR 2012 especially wants to promote cooperation between different research groups and will provide the participants with ample occasion for direct interaction.
